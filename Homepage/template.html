Arcane

Arcane is a critically acclaimed animated series set in the world of League of Legends, exploring the origins of iconic champions. The story delves into the tension between Piltover, a prosperous city of innovation, and Zaun, its struggling undercity. It centers on themes of family, betrayal, social inequality, and the consequences of ambition, focusing on the intertwined lives of its characters.

Main characters

Jinx

The Loose Cannon

Once an innocent and inventive child, Powder's trauma and insecurities pushed her into becoming Jinx, a chaotic and unpredictable criminal. Guided by Silco, she unleashes her destructive tendencies on Piltover and Zaun. Jinx embodies tragedy, as her fractured mind constantly battles memories of her past.

Vi

The Piltover Enforcer

A fierce and resilient fighter from Zaun, Vi is driven by her desire to protect those she loves. Orphaned at a young age, she grew up under Vander's care before becoming a street brawler. Her strong will and fists earned her the nickname "The Piltover Enforcer." She struggles with her sister Jinx's descent into madness.

Ekko

The Boy Savior

Known as "The Boy Savior," Ekko is a brilliant inventor and leader of the Firelights, a resistance group in Zaun. As a child, he was a close friend of Vi and Powder. Using his ingenuity and resourcefulness, he fights for a brighter future for his people while wielding technology to manipulate time.

Caitlyn

The Sheriff of Piltover

Born into a wealthy family in Piltover, Caitlyn is determined to uphold justice as "The Sheriff of Piltover." She partners with Vi to investigate the city's corruption. Despite her privileged background, she empathizes with the struggles of Zaun and seeks to bridge the gap between the two worlds.

Jayce

The Defender of Tomorrow

A visionary inventor hailed as "The Defender of Tomorrow," Jayce pioneered Hextech, a revolutionary technology in Piltover. Ambitious and idealistic, he dreams of a better world but grapples with the moral dilemmas of wielding immense power. His partnership with Viktor lays the foundation for Piltover's technological evolution.

Victor

The Machine Herald

A Zaunite scientist who becomes obsessed with advancing humanity through technology, Viktor works with Jayce to develop Hextech. His failing health and desperation lead him to radical experiments, transforming him into "The Machine Herald." He represents the conflict between progress and humanity.

Heimerdinger

The Revered Inventor

A yordle scientist and revered inventor, Heimerdinger has guided Piltover for centuries with his wisdom and innovation. Despite his good intentions, his cautious nature clashes with Jayce's ambition, creating tension as the city embraces Hextech's rapid development.

Silco

The Eye of Zaun

A cunning and manipulative leader, Silco aims to establish Zaun's independence by any means necessary. Once Vander's ally, their ideologies diverged after betrayal. As a surrogate father to Jinx, he exploits her instability to achieve his ambitions, embodying the complexity of villainy.

Vander

The Lion of Zaun

Known as "The Lion of Zaun," Vander was once a revolutionary leader fighting for Zaun's freedom. After tragedy struck, he chose peace and became a father figure to Vi and Powder. His selfless sacrifice to protect them lays the foundation for much of Arcane's conflict.
